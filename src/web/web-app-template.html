3 · Moat assets — capital that survives any model release

MOAT scores tasks as risk = 100 × automatable-shape × (1 − moat-strength), then projects forward assuming the frontier closes ~half of each task's automation gap every 5 years while each moat decays at its own rate (accountability slowest, embodiment fastest). Every constant is an explicit, arguable assumption — argue with it, then move your hours.
